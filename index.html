CV Assemblage

CV Assemblage interrogates the ontological frameworks that underpin institutional legitimacy and the commodification of academic labor. Each work, whether minimal or absurd, occupies a liminal space where curation becomes a performative actâ€”a critique of the hegemonic metrics that dictate scholarly and artistic validation.

Situated at the intersection of academia and praxis, this collective project mobilizes the discourse of art to destabilize entrenched paradigms. These works transcend mere objects, functioning as interstitial interventions that disrupt the dialectics of form and content, challenging the tenure dossier as an instrument of professional valorization.

In subverting the normative expectations of scholarly achievement, CV Assemblage posits a radical reimagining of the cultural capital assigned to artistic labor, underscoring the inherent absurdity within institutional rituals of recognition and validation. This project playfully critiques the tenure dossier as a superficial resume, where the perceived impact is often exaggerated for the sake of adding another line, highlighting the performative nature of self-advocacy within academic contexts.
